- Zero-one to one (0..1:1)
- One-one to one (1..1:1)

- Zero-n to one (0..n:1)

- Zero-star to one (0..*:1)
- n to one (n:1)
- N to one (N:1)
- Star to one (*:1)
- One-n to one (1..n:1)
- One-N to one (1..N:1)
- One-star to one (1..*:1)
